Visualizing Topography Changes


Scroll through the two terrain models to see how mountaintop removal mining has changed the topography of the region near Coal River Mountain. This mine is found at 37.8676, -81.5116. The first model was built from data in 2003, while the second model shows about 10 years of mining with data from 2014.

Data Sources: Historical elevation data compiled from USGS DEMs. Recent elevation based on LIDAR data from West Virginia Department of Environmental Protection GIS Server. Aerial photography from USGS.
